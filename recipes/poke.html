Poke

Description

Poke bowls seem complicated, but are deceptively simple to make! All you need is some fresh seafood and some leftover greens, and you have yourself a health and filling meal for the day.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 c. Low-sodium Soy Sauce
  • 2 tsp. Rice Vinegar
  • 2 tsp. Sesame Oil
  • 1 tsp. Fresly Grated Ginger
  • A pinch of crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2 Green Onions
  • 1 tsp. Toasted Sesame Seeds
  • 1 lb. Sushi-Grade Ahi Tuna, cut into bite-sized pieces
