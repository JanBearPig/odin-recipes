Sinigang

Description

Sinigang is a tamarind-based soup that has a sour, but refreshing taste. It's incredibly easy to make at home, making it a mainstay in Filipino households.

This recipe is to make Salmon Sinigang, but the meat can be easily replaced by pork or beef.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs. salmon fillet
  • 3 10g pouches Knorr Sinigang na Isda mix
  • 1 bunch kangkong
  • 10 pieces snake beans cut into 2 inch length
  • 2 medium ripe tomato sliced into wedges
  • 6 to 8 pieces okra
  • 6 ounces Daikon radish
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 4 pieces long green peppers
  • 3 tablesppons patis
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 quarts water

Steps

  1. Pour water in a cooking pot. Let boil.
  2. Add tomato, onion, and Daikon radish. Cover and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add salmon fillet. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add Knorr Sinigang na Isda mix. Stir.
  5. Put the long green peppers into the pot along with the okra and snake beans. Cover the pot. Cook in medium heat for 2 minutes.
  6. Add kankong stalks. Season with patis and ground black pepper. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
  7. Add kankong leaves. Turn the heat off. Cover the pot and let it stay for 5 minutes.
  8. Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve!
  9. Share and enjoy!
